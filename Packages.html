A Family Adventure
To Remember


Kenya is among our favourite destinations for a family safari, with wonderful guides, a great mix of activities tailored to suit all ages and a bounty of wildlife in beautiful locations.

This trip is a real family adventure, combining the freedom of a Laikipia ranch with a classic safari in the Maasai Mara. Look forward to learning how to track animals with a Maasai guide, exciting game drives, meeting local schoolchildren for a game of soccer, an epic helicopter flight over Mount Kenya, horse rides, mountain biking, and seeing the 'Big Five' and much more.
