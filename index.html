10 Day Trip

Mirissa, Sri Lanka

Mirissa is one of southern Sri Lanka's most popular beach getaways, with amazing surfing waves, and waterways abounding with marine life, from turtles to enormous blue whales, abound in this area. Spend your days lounging in your hammock with fresh coconuts, eating amazing food from coastal eateries, and zipping around on your motorcycle.

12 Day Trip

Santa Cristina Valgardena, Italia

Santa Cristina in Italy is described as a bustling community surrounded by lush meadows and set against the majestic mountain peaks of the Dolomites. The Adinae municipality, which is 1,428 meters above sea level, is also home to two protected areas: the Puez-Geisler Nature Park in the north, which includes Cislealm, and the Alpe di Siusi protected area in the south, which includes Monte Pana.
