Colombo
Sri Lanka

Colombo, the entrance to Sri Lanka's wonders, has a strong colonial legacy and is a melting pot of races, religions, and civilizations. It's a city of contrasts, with gleaming hotels and sophisticated office towers coexisting with magnificent red-tiled colonial-era structures. Aromatic spices, colorful silks, cottons, and fresh fruit and vegetables abound in the bustling street markets. Explore Colombo's multi-ethnic heritage, with churches, mosques, and temples sharing the same streets, and see prominent sights such as Fort Station, the Red Mosque, and the Khan clock tower. Plunge into Colombo's fascinating street food scene, dine like a native, and become immersed in the spinning Pettah Bazaar's sights, sounds, and fragrances.
