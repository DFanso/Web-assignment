Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China, also known as Wanli Changcheng in Chinese (Pinyin) or Wan-li Ch'ang-ch'eng in Wade-Giles romanization ("10,000-Li Long Wall"), is a massive fortification built in ancient China. It is one of the world's largest construction undertakings. The Great Wall is actually a series of walls built over millennia in northern China and southern Mongolia, many of them parallel to one another.
