Our Packages

11 Day Trip

Mirissa, Sri Lanka

Mirissa is one of southern Sri Lanka's most popular beach getaways, with amazing surfing waves, and waterways abounding with marine life, from turtles to enormous blue whales, abound in this area. Spend your days lounging in your hammock with fresh coconuts, eating amazing food from coastal eateries, and zipping around on your motorcycle.

11 Day Trip

Santa Cristina Valgardena, Italia

Santa Cristina in Italy is described as a bustling community surrounded by lush meadows and set against the majestic mountain peaks of the Dolomites. The Adinae municipality, which is 1,428 meters above sea level, is also home to two protected areas: the Puez-Geisler Nature Park in the north, which includes Cislealm, and the Alpe di Siusi protected area in the south, which includes Monte Pana.

11 Day Trip

Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

Sigiriya is known for its palace ruins, which are perched atop a large 200-meter-high rock and surrounded by the remnants of a vast network of gardens, reservoirs, and other facilities. The rock is actually a lava plug from a long-since-extinct volcano.

11 Day Trip

Puebla, Mexico

Puebla is Mexico's fourth most populous and oldest metropolis. It is well-known among Mexicans for its great gastronomy, magnificent churches, and vibrant cultural scene... If it's beautiful colonial cathedrals you're looking for, Puebla has more than you could see in a year.

11 Day Trip

Indonesia

Indonesia is known for its various and multicultural islands, which range from the extremely religious Aceh in the north through Java, the country's administrative capital, to the tropical paradise of Bali, and all the way down to the province of Papua on the border with independent Papua New Guinea.

11 Day Trip

Colmar, France

The city is known for its well-preserved old town, several architectural sites, and museums, one of which holds the Isenheim Altarpiece, the Unterlinden Museum. Colmar is the "capital of Alsatian wine" (capitale des vins d'Alsace), and it is located on the Alsatian Wine Route.
